Welcome to my homepage!

I am a theoretical chemist interested in understanding the structural and dynamical properties complex systems such as supercooled liquids, polymorphic systems. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the polymer theory group headed by Prof. Dr. Kurt Kremer at the Max Planck Institute for Polymer Research in Mainz, Germany. Within the group, I closely collaborate with Dr. Joe Rudzinski, Dr. Tristan Bereau (now at University of Amsterdam) and Dr. Robin Cortes-Huerto .

Before coming to Mainz, I was a postdoc at the Cambridge University, United Kingdom. I worked with Prof David Wales. I got my PhD from the CSIR National Chemical Laboratory, Pune, India. My PhD supervisor was Dr Sarika Maitra Bhattacharyya.
